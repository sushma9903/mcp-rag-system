# Company Internal Policies and FAQs

## 1. Remote Work Policy

Employees are allowed to work remotely up to three days per week, subject to manager approval. 
Remote work is intended to support flexibility while maintaining collaboration and productivity.

Remote employees are expected to:
- Be available during core working hours (10 AM to 4 PM IST)
- Attend all mandatory meetings
- Ensure a stable internet connection

Requests for fully remote roles are evaluated on a case-by-case basis.

---

## 2. Leave Policy

The company provides the following types of leave:

- **Casual Leave**: 12 days per year
- **Sick Leave**: 10 days per year
- **Earned Leave**: 15 days per year

Sick leave exceeding two consecutive days may require a medical certificate. 
Unused earned leave can be carried forward to the next year, subject to company limits.

---

## 3. Information Security Policy

All employees must adhere to information security best practices to protect company data.

Key guidelines include:
- Do not share credentials or access tokens
- Use company-approved devices for official work
- Report suspicious emails or security incidents immediately

Confidential data must not be stored on personal devices without encryption.

---

## 4. Code of Conduct

Employees are expected to maintain professional behavior at all times.

Unacceptable behavior includes:
- Harassment or discrimination of any kind
- Sharing confidential information externally
- Misuse of company resources

Violations of the code of conduct may lead to disciplinary action.

---

## 5. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

### What are the core working hours?
Core working hours are from 10 AM to 4 PM IST on all working days.

### Can I carry forward unused leave?
Only earned leave can be carried forward. Casual and sick leave cannot be carried forward.

### Is remote work allowed full-time?
Full-time remote work is not the default policy and requires special approval.

### What should I do if I suspect a security breach?
Immediately inform the IT or security team and avoid further interaction with the suspicious system or email.
